Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Welcome to our Fitness & Nutrition App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, date of birth, gender
  • Health & Fitness Data: Height, weight, fitness goals, workout preferences, activity level
  • Nutrition Data: Food logs, meal plans, dietary preferences
  • Workout Data: Exercise history, workout sessions, progress tracking
  • Profile Photos: Images you upload to your profile

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized workout and nutrition recommendations
  • Track your fitness progress and achievements
  • Send workout reminders and motivational notifications
  • Generate AI-powered meal plans and exercise routines
  • Analyze food images for nutritional information

3. Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Data is stored securely using Firebase Cloud Firestore, images are stored on Cloudinary, and all transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Storage)
  • Cloudinary (Image Processing)
  • Google Gemini AI & GitHub Models (Chatbot)
  • FatSecret API & Clarifai (Food Recognition)
  • CalorieNinja API (Nutrition Lookup)

5. AI Data Processing

When using AI-powered features, certain data is processed by third-party AI services. Chatbot conversations are sent to Google Gemini AI, and food images are processed by Clarifai/FatSecret. AI providers may retain data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share information only with your explicit consent, to comply with legal obligations, or with service providers under strict confidentiality agreements.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to access your personal data, update or correct it, delete your account, and export your workout/nutrition data at any time through the app settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the Support & Help section in the app.
