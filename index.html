Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a personalized workout plan?

Complete the fitness survey during signup to receive a customized 4-week workout program based on your goals, fitness level, and available equipment.

How accurate is the food image recognition?

Our AI-powered food recognition uses FatSecret and Clarifai to identify foods from images, with CalorieNinja providing nutritional data. While generally accurate, we recommend verifying nutritional information for best results.

What AI technologies does this app use?

We use Google Gemini AI for meal plan generation and our nutrition chatbot. GitHub Models serves as a fallback AI service. Clarifai and FatSecret power our food image recognition. All AI recommendations are guidance only and not medical advice.

How does the AI handle my custom allergies?

If you add a custom allergy that the AI doesn't recognize, it will err on the side of caution and may avoid potentially related foods. Always verify meal plans if you have serious allergies.

Is my data shared with AI services?

Yes, when using AI features, relevant data (like your health profile, dietary preferences, and chatbot messages) is sent to third-party AI services (Google Gemini, GitHub Models) to generate personalized responses. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I change my workout reminder time?

Yes! Go to Profile > Notification Settings to customize your workout reminder time and notification preferences.

How do I track my progress?

Use the Progress tab to view your workout history, nutrition logs, weight trends, and achievement statistics over time.

What if I can't complete a workout?

You can modify exercises, skip exercises, or choose a different difficulty level. Listen to your body and adjust as needed.

Should I follow AI meal plans without consulting a doctor?

No. AI-generated meal plans and nutrition advice are for informational purposes only. Always consult with a healthcare professional, especially if you have medical conditions, take medications, or have specific dietary needs.

How do I delete my account?

Currently, you can sign out through Profile settings. To permanently delete your account and data, please contact our support team.
